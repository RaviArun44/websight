Admission Process

Undergraduate (B.E./B.Tech) admissions are conducted through Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) counselling based on 10+2 marks. No entrance exam required.

Postgraduate (M.E.) admissions are through TANCA or consortium as applicable.

Undergraduate Programs (B.E. / B.Tech)

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • Mechanical Engineering (MECH)
  • Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • Civil Engineering (CIVIL)
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science (AI & DS)

Postgraduate Programs (M.E.)

  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Embedded Systems Technologies
  • High Voltage Engineering
  • Energy Engineering
  • Information Technology (Information and Cyber Warfare)

Documents Required at the Time of Admission

  • Provisional Allotment Order from TNEA 2025 (Color Printout)
  • 10th Mark Sheet
  • 11th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate & Conduct Certificate
  • Permanent Community Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • Income Certificate (for scholarship)
  • 4 Passport Size Photographs
  • Student Aadhaar Card Copy
  • Two sets of photocopies of all certificates

For First Graduate Students: First Graduate Certificate & Joint Declaration.
