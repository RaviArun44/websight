History & Establishment

National Engineering College (NEC) was established in 1984 as a self-financing institution sanctioned by the Government of Tamil Nadu. Founded by Kalvithanthai Thiru. K. Ramasamy, a visionary philanthropist, NEC transformed barren land into a premier academic complex in Kovilpatti, Thoothukudi District.

NEC is an autonomous institution affiliated to Anna University, Chennai, approved by AICTE, accredited by NBA (Tier-1), and NAAC with B++ grade.

Vision

Transforming lives through quality education and research with human values.

Mission

  • Maintain excellent infrastructure and highly qualified, dedicated faculty.
  • Provide a conducive environment fostering humanity, wisdom, creativity, and team spirit.
  • Promote ethical behavior and commitment to society.
  • Partner with academic, industrial, and government entities for collaborative research.
